ABOUT US

Mytutor.lk is an online tutor directory that connects students and parents with tutors/ teachers across Sri Lanka. As well it is the number one tuition class advertising platform in Sri Lanka and it is a smart and easy way to find tutors/ teachers quickly on an online platform. Mytutor.lk was found in 2011 and now Mytutor.lk has expanded its business into several other sections and committed to facilitating the education in Sri Lanka while achieving the best education portal which comes to every doorstep. Thus, along with the core concept of empowering education sector including higher education, Mytutor.lk works for career empowerment too.


Mytutor.lk is open for students, parents, tutors, job seekers, educational institutions and anyone who seeks for educational aids.
